Before You Email

Including the details below helps us troubleshoot faster:

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The app version, if available.
  • A short description of what happened and when.
  • For purchase issues, the approximate purchase date and item purchased. Do not send full payment details.

Purchases, Coins, Hearts, and Refunds

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Coins, hearts, retries, rewards, and other in-app items are digital game items only and have no cash value.

Refunds: Refund requests for App Store purchases must be submitted through Apple. We cannot directly issue App Store refunds, but we can help troubleshoot missing items.

If you bought coins and they did not appear, fully close and reopen the App. If they are still missing, contact support with the date of purchase and the item purchased.

Ads and Rewarded Ads

The App may show ads through Google AdMob. Some ads may be rewarded ads, such as watching an optional ad for a retry, heart, or similar in-game reward.

Rewarded ads are optional. Rewards are granted only after the ad provider confirms completion. Rewards may not be available if no ad is available, the ad is closed early, your connection fails, or the ad provider does not confirm completion.

Frequently Asked Questions

I bought coins but did not receive them.

Coins should be delivered after Apple confirms the purchase. Fully close and reopen the App. If the coins are still missing, email support with the date of purchase and the item purchased.

Can I transfer coins, hearts, or progress to another device?

Progress and certain items may be stored locally on your device unless the App specifically offers cloud sync or restoration. Deleting the App, resetting your device, or switching devices may cause local progress or earned items to be lost.

Can I redeem coins for money?

No. Coins and other in-app items are for gameplay only. They have no cash value and cannot be sold, transferred, traded, redeemed, or exchanged for money.

How do hearts work?

Hearts are lives used during gameplay. Depending on the App version, hearts may refill over time, be earned through gameplay, be granted through optional rewarded ads, or be obtained with coins.

How do I remove ads?

If an ad-free purchase or subscription is offered in the App, the App will describe what it includes before purchase. If no ad-free option is shown, then an ad-free option is not currently available.

Is the App for children?

The App is not directed to children under 13. Parents and guardians should review the Privacy Policy and device settings for purchases, tracking, and ad personalization controls.
