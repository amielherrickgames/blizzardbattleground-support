Player Support

Frostillery!

Need help with a bug, purchase, ad issue, or gameplay question? Send a support request and include the details below so the issue can be reviewed quickly.

Email Support

Support email: amielherrick.games@gmail.com

What to Include

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your device model, such as iPhone 17 Pro Max or iPhone 15.
  • Your iOS version.
  • The Frostillery! app version, if visible in the app or App Store.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected to happen.
  • The level, screen, cannon, tank skin, upgrade, or purchase involved.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if they help explain the issue.

Purchase Help

Frostillery! may offer optional in-app purchases, such as coin packs or virtual items. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

If a purchase does not appear, first restart the app and confirm the purchase in your Apple purchase history. If the issue continues, email support with the approximate purchase date, item purchased, and a screenshot of the Apple receipt.

Do not email credit card numbers, Apple ID passwords, or bank information.

Ads

Frostillery! may show ads, including interstitial ads between gameplay moments. Ads help support ongoing development and updates.

If an ad freezes, does not close, repeats too often, or appears in a confusing place, please email support with your device model, iOS version, app version, and what you were doing when the ad appeared.

Gameplay and Progress

If level progress, coins, upgrades, skins, or settings do not look correct, restart the app first. If the issue continues, send the level number, what changed, and any screenshots you have.

For best results, keep Frostillery! updated through the App Store so you receive bug fixes and balance updates.

Common Questions

The game crashed or froze. What should I do?

  1. Close and reopen Frostillery!.
  2. Check the App Store for an update.
  3. Restart your device if the issue continues.
  4. Email support if the same issue happens again.

Can I get a refund for an in-app purchase?

Apple controls App Store payment processing and refund decisions. You can request a refund through Apple’s report-a-problem process or your Apple purchase history. Support can help troubleshoot delivery issues but cannot directly access your payment card or Apple account.

Why am I seeing ads?

Ads help keep Frostillery! available and support future updates. The game may show ads between levels or at other natural breaks in gameplay.

How do I report a privacy question?

Email amielherrick.games@gmail.com with the subject line Frostillery Privacy Question. You can also review the Privacy Policy.
