Purchases, coins, and refunds

Blizzard Battleground may offer optional in-app purchases, such as coin packs or other in-game items. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card number, Apple Account password, or other Apple billing credentials.

  • Coin packs and similar items: These are generally consumable digital items, which means they are intended to be used in the app after purchase.
  • Missing purchase: Fully close and reopen the app first. If the purchase is still missing, contact support with the approximate purchase date and item purchased.
  • Refund requests: Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple’s Report a Problem page.

Ads and rewarded ads

The app may show ads through Google AdMob. Some ads may be personalized depending on your device settings, consent choices, region, and Apple’s App Tracking Transparency permission status.

  • Rewarded ads are optional: When offered, you choose whether to watch a rewarded ad in exchange for an in-game benefit, such as a retry, heart, or similar reward.
  • Ad choices: You can manage app tracking permissions in iOS Settings and adjust Apple advertising settings under Privacy & Security.
  • Consent notices: In some regions, the app or AdMob may present consent options before serving personalized ads.

Privacy and data requests

The app is designed to avoid collecting personal information directly from players. However, third-party services such as Apple and Google AdMob may process information needed for purchases, ads, fraud prevention, diagnostics, analytics, or app functionality.

Read the full Privacy Policy for details about data collection, third-party services, advertising identifiers, children’s privacy, privacy rights, and how to contact us.

Children and parents

Blizzard Battleground is not intended to be directed to children under 13. Parents and guardians should review the app’s age rating, device settings, purchase controls, and privacy settings before allowing a child to play.

  • Use Apple’s Screen Time and purchase approval settings to help manage downloads, in-app purchases, and app access.
  • If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request, contact us so we can address it.
  • Do not include sensitive personal information in support emails unless it is necessary to resolve the issue.

Frequently asked questions

I bought coins but didn’t receive them.

Coins are usually delivered after Apple confirms the purchase. Fully close and reopen the app. If the coins still do not appear, email support with the approximate purchase date, item purchased, and any non-sensitive receipt details. Do not send your Apple Account password or full payment information.

How do hearts work?

Hearts represent lives or retry opportunities. Depending on the version of the app, hearts may refill over time, be earned through gameplay, be obtained through an optional rewarded ad, or be purchased with in-game currency.

Are rewarded ads required?

No. Rewarded ads are intended to be optional. If you choose to watch one, the app will tell you what reward is being offered before you decide.

How do I remove or limit ads?

If an ad-free option is available in your version of the app, it will appear inside the app. You can also limit tracking through iOS Settings. Some non-personalized or contextual ads may still appear depending on the app version and region.

I changed phones. Will my progress carry over?

Unless the app version specifically supports cloud sync or account-based progress, gameplay progress may be stored locally on your device. Contact support if you believe progress was lost after an update or device change.

How do I report a bug?

Email support with your device model, iOS version, app version, what you were doing when the issue happened, and the steps that seem to trigger it. Screenshots are helpful when available.
