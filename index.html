Frequently Asked

I bought coins but didn’t receive them.

Coins are delivered as soon as Apple confirms your purchase. If they don’t appear, fully close and reopen the app. If they’re still missing, email us with the date of purchase and we’ll sort it out.

How do hearts work?

Hearts are your lives. You spend one when you lose a level. They refill over time, and you can also earn them by watching an ad or spending coins.

How do I remove ads?

An ad-free option is planned for a future update. In the meantime, the rewarded ads (for hearts and free retries) are always optional — you only watch them if you choose to.

I changed phones — will my progress carry over?

Progress is saved on your device. Cross-device sync is something we’re considering for a future update.
