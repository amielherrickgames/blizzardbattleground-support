Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Blizzard Battleground, developed by Amiel Herrick (“we,” “us,” or “our”), handles information when you use the App.

Effective date: June 13, 2026  |  Last updated: June 13, 2026

Plain-language summary: The App does not require you to create an account and we do not directly collect your name, email address, or payment card details to let you play. However, the App may use third-party services such as Google AdMob and Apple App Store services that may collect data as described below.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS game Blizzard Battleground and related support pages. It does not apply to websites, platforms, or services operated by third parties, including Apple or Google, except where we describe our use of those services in the App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name or email address to play the App. However, the App may involve the following categories of information:

  • Advertising data: Google AdMob may collect or receive device information, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, advertising identifiers, ad interactions, and related data used to provide, measure, limit, and improve ads.
  • Device and usage data: The App or third-party services may collect app interactions, gameplay events, device type, operating system version, app version, language, region, and performance data.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs, performance data, and similar technical information may be collected to identify bugs and improve reliability.
  • Purchase information: Apple processes in-app purchases. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We may receive limited transaction confirmation information needed to deliver purchased items or troubleshoot purchase issues.
  • Support communications: If you email us, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message, screenshots, and purchase troubleshooting details.

3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Display, personalize, measure, limit, and improve advertising, where permitted.
  • Provide optional rewarded ads and confirm reward eligibility.
  • Process and verify in-app purchases through Apple.
  • Diagnose crashes, bugs, fraud, abuse, or technical problems.
  • Respond to support, privacy, copyright, or legal requests.
  • Comply with applicable law, platform rules, and enforcement requests.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that operate under their own privacy policies and terms:

  • Google AdMob: Used to show ads, including optional rewarded ads. Google may process data for ad serving, frequency capping, fraud prevention, measurement, and personalization where allowed.
  • Apple App Store and In-App Purchase: Used to distribute the App and process purchases. Apple handles payment processing and refund workflows.
  • Apple device and iOS services: iOS settings may control tracking permissions, Apple advertising settings, purchase restrictions, and other device-level privacy choices.

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices. Please review Google’s and Apple’s privacy policies for more information.

5. Advertising, App Tracking Transparency, and Your Choices

The App may show ads through Google AdMob. Ads may be contextual or personalized depending on your device settings, region, consent choices, and applicable law.

  • App Tracking Transparency: On iOS, the App may request permission before tracking your activity across other companies’ apps and websites for advertising or measurement purposes. You can manage this through iOS Settings.
  • Apple advertising settings: You can manage Apple advertising personalization through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.
  • AdMob consent: In certain regions, such as the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, the App may ask for consent before using certain data for personalized ads or accessing device storage where legally required.
  • Privacy choices: If the App provides a Privacy Choices or Manage Consent option, you can use it to review or change available ad consent selections.

6. In-App Purchases

Purchases, including coin packs or other digital items, are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card number. Apple may provide us with limited transaction information needed to validate purchases, deliver digital items, prevent fraud, and troubleshoot support requests.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address the issue.

Parents and guardians can use iOS parental controls, purchase restrictions, App Tracking Transparency controls, and advertising settings to manage a child’s app experience.

8. Privacy Rights and Requests

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of certain personal information, or to object to or opt out of certain processing, including targeted advertising or sale/sharing where applicable.

Because we do not maintain user accounts and do not directly store most app usage or advertising data, we may not be able to identify data associated with a particular user unless you provide enough information to verify and process the request. Where data is controlled by a third party, such as Apple or Google, we may direct you to that third party’s privacy tools or request process.

9. Data Retention

We retain support emails and related records only as long as reasonably necessary for support, legal, safety, fraud prevention, and business purposes. Data collected by Apple, Google, or other third-party services is retained according to their own policies.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures intended to protect information we handle. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Users

The App and third-party services may process information in the United States and other countries. Privacy laws may differ from those in your location.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date or last updated date and post the revised policy on this page.
