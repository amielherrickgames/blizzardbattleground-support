Privacy Policy

Blizzard Battleground · Effective date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Blizzard Battleground (the “App”), developed by Amiel Herrick (“we,” “us,” or “our”), handles information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

Blizzard Battleground does not ask you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name or email to play. However, the App uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect your device’s IP address (which may be used to estimate general location), advertising identifiers, device information, and data about your interactions with ads.
  • Diagnostic and performance data: Crash logs, app performance data, and similar diagnostic information may be collected to maintain and improve the App. This data may also be used for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • Purchase information: In-app purchases (coin packs) are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment details; Apple handles all payment processing.

How Information Is Used

Information collected through these services is used to display advertising, process in-app purchases, diagnose technical problems, and improve the App’s performance and features.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information
  • Apple App Store (in-app purchases) — Apple Privacy Policy

Personalized Advertising and Your Choices

Ads shown in the App may be personalized based on advertising identifiers. You can limit ad personalization through your device settings (for example, on iOS via Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising, and by managing app tracking permissions). You may also reset or limit your device’s advertising identifier at any time.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

Data Retention and Your Rights

We do not directly store personal data. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies. You may request information about, or deletion of, data associated with you by contacting us at the email below; where data is held by a third party, we will direct you to the appropriate process.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
