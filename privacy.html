Overview

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Frostillery! is a mobile game published by Amiel Herrick. The game is designed for casual entertainment and may include advertisements, gameplay progression, virtual coins or items, and optional in-app purchases.

This policy applies to Frostillery! on iOS and to related support communications. It does not apply to websites, apps, or services operated by third parties except where described below.

Frostillery! does not require users to create an account, and the developer does not collect credit card numbers or other payment card details.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the game, the following categories of information may be collected by the app, by Apple, by Google AdMob, or by other service providers used to operate the game:

Game and device information

  • Device type, operating system, app version, language, and general device settings.
  • Gameplay activity, such as level progress, coins, upgrades, skins, settings, and similar in-game status information.
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, performance data, and bug-related information.

Advertising information

  • Advertising identifiers, such as Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers, if permission is granted through iOS.
  • Approximate location inferred from IP address, ad interaction data, and other information used by advertising providers to deliver, measure, and improve ads.

Purchase information

  • Apple may process in-app purchases and provide transaction status or receipt information so the game can deliver purchased coins, items, or entitlements.
  • The developer does not receive your full payment card number, banking information, or Apple ID password.

Support information

  • If you email support, we may receive your email address, message, screenshots, device details you choose to provide, and any other information included in your request.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Operate the game and save or restore gameplay status where supported.
  • Display ads and measure ad performance.
  • Process in-app purchases and deliver virtual items.
  • Fix bugs, troubleshoot crashes, improve performance, and develop future updates.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, invalid ad traffic, or misuse of the app.
  • Comply with legal, tax, accounting, App Store, and platform obligations.

Ads and Google AdMob

Frostillery! may show ads through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect and use information from your device to provide ads, limit repetitive ads, detect invalid activity, measure ad performance, and support personalized or non-personalized advertising depending on your settings, location, and consent choices.

On iOS, you may be asked whether you allow tracking for advertising purposes. If you do not allow tracking, the game may still show ads, but those ads may be less personalized.

You can learn more about Google’s privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy and about Google’s ad technologies at Google Advertising Policies and Technologies.

In-App Purchases

Frostillery! may offer optional in-app purchases, such as coin packs, upgrades, skins, or other virtual items. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment information and may provide the app with confirmation that a transaction was completed so the purchased item can be delivered.

In-app purchases are subject to Apple’s terms, payment rules, refund policies, and account settings. For purchase or refund issues, you may need to contact Apple Support or use Apple’s purchase history tools.

How Information Is Shared

Information may be shared with:

  • Google AdMob and advertising partners to deliver, measure, and improve ads and detect invalid traffic.
  • Apple to process in-app purchases, App Store distribution, diagnostics, and platform services.
  • Service providers that help operate, support, secure, analyze, or improve the game.
  • Legal or safety authorities if required by law or needed to protect rights, safety, users, or the integrity of the game.

We do not sell your personal information directly to data brokers. Advertising providers may process data according to their own policies and your consent choices.

Your Choices

  • Tracking permission: You can manage app tracking choices in iOS Settings.
  • Ad personalization: You can manage some Google ad personalization settings through your Google account and device settings.
  • Purchases: You can manage subscriptions, purchase history, refunds, and family purchase controls through Apple’s App Store and Apple ID settings.
  • Support emails: You may ask us to delete support emails unless retention is needed for legal, security, or dispute-resolution reasons.
  • App removal: You can stop further app-based collection by deleting Frostillery! from your device.

Children

Frostillery! is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request or other communication, please contact us so we can review and delete the information where appropriate.

Retention and Security

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including support, troubleshooting, legal compliance, accounting, security, and platform requirements. Third-party providers, including Google and Apple, may retain information according to their own policies.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect support communications and app-related records within our control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at this page.
